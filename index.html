About AviatorDan

A little bit about me...

Hi, I'm Dan. I'm the avgeek behind AviatorDan.

I started my first YouTube channel in 2017. The name and branding changed a few times, but in 2020 something finally clicked. I came up with the name AviatorDan and built a new plan around it.

From 2020 to 2022, the channel reached millions of views and grew into a brilliant community of aviation and gaming enthusiasts.

Then life got busy. I joined the Infinite Flight team, focused on work and spent a few years deciding what the next chapter of AviatorDan should look like.

Now it is 2026, and it is time to get the show on the road again.

The plan is simple. Create content when I can, enjoy making it and grow the community step by step. Aviation and community are both a huge part of who I am, and I cannot wait to see where this goes.

Aside from the content stuff, I love to travel, go to the gym, and spend time with family!

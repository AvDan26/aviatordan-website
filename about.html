About AviatorDan

The story behind the channel, the break, and the flight plan for what comes next.

Hi, I’m Dan.

The avgeek behind AviatorDan.

Hi everyone, I’m Dan, the avgeek behind AviatorDan.

The evolution of my YouTube channel is long, so I’ll keep this short. Back in 2017, I decided to start my very first YouTube channel — the same one I use today. Over the years it evolved, and the channel name and branding changed on multiple occasions.

In 2020, something clicked: a whole new name and game plan. I decided to roll with the name AviatorDan, and ever since then it stuck. Sure, I’m not a pilot, but the name had a special ring to it that was catchy.

From 2020 to 2022, I took my channel to new heights, gaining millions of views and building a great community of aviation and gaming enthusiasts. Then came the big break — and a massive opportunity for me.

After 2022, life caught up with me. Not in a bad way; I was busy with other cool things, like joining the Infinite Flight team. How cool is that? Between life and work, I fell into a deep hole for four years trying to work out what to do with AviatorDan — something that is such a big part of me.

Fast-forward to 2026: it’s time to get the show on the road again. I’m back, but this time with one simple plan — create content when I’m able, and enjoy doing it.

I want to grow the community even more, step by step. My joint passion for community and aviation is powerful, and I can’t wait to see where it goes.
